Earned Income TaxThe Earned Income Tax (EIT) rate in Neshannock Township is 1%. Earned Income Tax is the 1% tax you pay on your wages earned to the community where you live.Employers are responsible for having all employees fill out a "Residency Certification Form", which includes Political Subdivision District (PSD) Codes for the community where you live, as well as the community where you work. The PSD code for Neshannock Township is 370401. PSD codes for communities can be found on the PA Department of Community & Economic Development website.Payments Due: Berkheimer and Associates, P.O.
