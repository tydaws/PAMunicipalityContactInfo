SOUTH LEBANON TOWNSHIPPayments for sewer bills, pavilion rentals, fines and parking space rent can be placed in the drop box on the wall in front of the Township Building. If you would like a receipt mailed to you, please indicate as such.  Questions or any further comments can be made by phone at 717-274-0481, email (sltownship@comcast.net) or fill out this form and you will receive a reply within 24-48 hours.  If you need to speak to someone in person, enter the building during our normal business hours between 7:30 a.m. and 4:00 p.m., Monday - Friday.
