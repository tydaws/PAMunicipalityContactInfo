Planned Horseshoe Pike Closure 

Beginning Monday, June 19, 2023, and for a period of two weeks, Horseshoe Pike will be closed to all traffic between Gendry Drive and Edges Mill Road. This road closure is necessary to install a sewer serving Mi-Place in Caln Township.				Read on...
