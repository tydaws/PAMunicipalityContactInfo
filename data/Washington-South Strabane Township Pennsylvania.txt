Summer Materials Bid InformationPosted on: April 6, 2021 - 2:18pm





 


 

SOUTH STRABANE TOWNSHIPNOTICE TO BIDDERSNOTICE is hereby given that sealed bids for public works materials and gasoline/diesel fuel will be received by South Strabane Township ("Township"), Washington County, at the Municipal Building, 550 Washington Rd. | Washington, PA | 15301 for the following:Sealed bids will be received by the Township until 2:00 p.m. local time, Wednesday, April 19, 2022, at which time bids will be opened and read aloud in the Township Board Meeting Room at the Municipal Building.  Bids must be upon forms furnished by the Township and must be accompanied by a certified check or bid bond in the amount of 10% of the bid made payable to “South Strabane Township”.  Bidders on regular gasoline (pump price) must be on the PA State Wex Fleet Card Program. The Township reserves the right to reject any and/or all proposals.  All bid documents are available at the front counter of the Municipal Building.
