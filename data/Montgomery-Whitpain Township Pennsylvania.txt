Our Mission
The mission of Whitpain Township Government is to efficiently, responsibly, and creatively provide the essential services that build value, enhance quality of life, and protect the health, safety, and rights for all who live in, work in, and enjoy Whitpain.


Vision
Whitpain Township is a community that embraces the future while cherishing our heritage. We will continue to be recognized for our diversity, responsibility, and leadership in resource conservation. We shall never waver in our collective commitment to ensuring public safety and enhancing the quality of life of all of our fellow citizens.
 






  
 
 

 















Our Values
Whitpain Township Government seeks excellence in accomplishing every facet of our mission. In pursuit of excellence, we value most of all:
 






  
 
 















Integrity: We follow moral and ethical principles as we conduct our work. We are consistent in our deeds, methods, and expectations.


Stewardship: We value our natural, financial, and human resources and are diligent in protecting, preserving, and enhancing Whitpain Township.
 




Responsiveness: We respond quickly but thoughtfully to individuals, situations, and events. 


Respect: We treat everyone with respect. Our actions and words will demonstrate consideration and appreciation of others.
 




Partnership: We establish transparent, long-term relationships that are based on clarity, trust, shared goals, and open communication with organizations that are committed to improving Whitpain Township.
