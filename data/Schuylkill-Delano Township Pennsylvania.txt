Contact









If you are looking for help, dial 211 from anywhere in Pennsylvania. You will speak with a contact specialist who will help you locate programs and services in your area. The specialist can help make it easier for you to find information, discover options or deal with a crisis.
If you are having difficulties using the 211 number, dial 1-855-567-5341.



Outside of Pennsylvania
Dial 1-855-567-5341To find a 211 center in another state, go to www.211.org.

Feedback
Use the form below to contact us about partnerships, send us your comments or report any problems you experienced finding information on our website. We read all feedback carefully, but please note that we cannot respond to all comments submitted. 
If you are a resource provider wanting to add or update your listing, please use this form.
