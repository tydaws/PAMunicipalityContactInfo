Welcome to Shrewsbury Township!

POLITICAL SIGN REGULATIONS
The season is upon us, and campaign signs are starting to appear everywhere, which also brings an increase in sign complaints.
Campaign/Political Signs are “temporary” signs and are regulated as are ALL “temporary” signs. As a reminder, please keep the following in mind:


4 is maximum number of signs permitted per property.


6 sq. ft. is the maximum size of each sign.


Cannot be placed within a road right-of-way (minimum 10 ft. from edge of paved road is a good rule to follow).


Must be on private property with owners’ permission.


Signs in violation can be removed without notice and discarded, and a fine can be issued.
