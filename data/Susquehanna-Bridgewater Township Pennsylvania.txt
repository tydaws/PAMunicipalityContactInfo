How do I contact the Tax Collector for tax information?		

Dawn Arnold may be contacted at 570-432-0178 or bridgewatertwptaxcollector@gmail.com. For more information, please visit the staff directory.Tax Collection page.

How do I contact the Tax Collector for tax information?

Dawn Arnold may be contacted at 570-432-0178 or bridgewatertwptaxcollector@gmail.com. For more information, please visit the staff directory. Tax Collection page.




2.
Does Bridgewater Township issue fireworks permits?

No, Bridgewater Township does not issue or approve fireworks permits for individual use.
