EAST BUFFALO TOWNSHIP AND BUFFALO VALLEY RAIL TRAIL
TRAFFIC SIGNAL UPGRADES
MULTIMODAL TRANSPORTATION FUND GRANT
BID PROPOSAL AND CONTRACT DOCUMENTS
BIDS DUE:
TUESDAY, JUNE 6, 2023 AT 1:00 PM

NOTICE
On behalf of the East Buffalo Township Board of Supervisors and the Union County Trail Authority, East Buffalo Township, Union County, Pennsylvania, 589 Fairground Road, Lewisburg, PA, 17837, will accept sealed proposals until 1:00 PM, prevailing time, Tuesday, June 6, 2023 for the following in East Buffalo Township:

Market Street/Old Turnpike Road (SR 0045) and Fairground Road (SR 2007) Intersection Traffic Signal Replacement; and
Buffalo Valley Rail Trail (BVRT) crossing of Fairground Road – Rectangular Rapid Flashing Beacon (RRFB) Installation.

All bid documents, construction plans and specifications are available at the offices of East Buffalo Township, 589 Fairground Road, Lewisburg, PA, 17837, from 7:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m., Monday through Friday, except holidays and at www.ebtwp.org.
All bid proposals shall be properly executed in ink without alterations and shall be complete.
All bid proposals must be accompanied by a certified check or bid bond in the amount of ten percent (10%) of the bid. The successful bidder will be required to post performance and payment bonds in the amount of one hundred percent (100%) of the bid price.
Bid Proposals will be publicly opened at 1:15 PM on Tuesday, June 6, 2023 at the East Buffalo Township office located at 589 Fairground Road, Lewisburg, PA, 17837.
Unless all bids are rejected, the lowest responsible bidder may be selected by the respective municipalities at public meetings as follows:

East Buffalo Township Board of Supervisors will meet on Monday, June 12, 2023 at 5:30 PM at 589 Fairground Road, Lewisburg, PA, 17837.

Pennsylvania prevailing wages under the Pennsylvania Prevailing Wage Act are required. Refer to the Bureau of Labor Law Compliance Prevailing Wages Serial Number 20-00048.
The municipalities reserve the right to reject any or all bids or parts thereof in the best interest of the municipalities.
