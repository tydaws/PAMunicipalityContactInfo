Bastress: Basic Facts & Information
Bastress is a populated place located within the Township of Bastress, a minor civil division (MCD) of Lycoming County.
 The elevation of Bastress is 1,181 feet. Bastress appears on the Linden U.S. Geological Survey Map. Lycoming County is in the Eastern Time Zone (UTC -5 hours).
