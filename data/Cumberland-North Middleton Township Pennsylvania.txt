News Flash








North Middleton Township Draft Comprehensive Plan - Revised 

The revised Draft Comprehensive Plan will be on the agenda for the May 16, 2023, Planning Commission meeting.  The meeting will begin at 6:00 pm at the North Middleton Township Municipal Building.				Read on...




Draft Post Road Improvement Study 

The Post Road Improvement Study Committee's Consultant will be presenting the draft Post Road Improvement Study to the Board of Supervisors at their May 18, 2023 regular meeting beginning at 6:00 pm.				Read on...




Republic Services Memorial Day Collection Schedule 

Due to Memorial Day on Monday, May 29, 2023, trash and recycling collection will be pushed back one day.  If your regular pickup day is Monday, your trash and recycling will be picked up on Tuesday.				Read on...
