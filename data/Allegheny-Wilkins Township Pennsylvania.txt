Community Center






The Township maintains a beautiful Community Center that provides the perfect place for your next event no matter the weather. The building, located behind the Municipal Building on Peffer Road, is available for rental to Township residents at a cost of $150, not to exceed seven hours. A $100 refundable security deposit is also required at time of application.The Community Center includes one large room (32’X52’), one smaller room (22’x25’), a fully equipped kitchen, restroom facilities, an outdoor area and plenty of parking. The building has a total capacity of 148 using chairs and tables and 317 using chairs only. Additional information can be obtained by calling the Township office or by clicking Community Center Rules and Regulations.Community Center rentals require a permit which can be obtained at the Municipal Building or by clicking on the Community Center Rental Application below.Community Center Rental ApplicationCommunity Center Rules and RegulationsThe Community Center is under construction.  During Construction the small room will not be available to rent.  The large room has some minor construction while a door is replaced.  The large room is still available to rent during construction.Photos of Door Construction in Large Room:
